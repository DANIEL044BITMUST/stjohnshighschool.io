Footbal Team

The St John's Football team is managed by Coach Brian. The team has won alot of trophies like Mukono District Championship,Central Region Championship e.t.c

 
 

First NetBall Team

This team has always been the best netball team within Mukono District. It constitutes of girls who a mostly strong, tall and have the talent. It has won many trophies and competitions in Mukono District.

 
 

Cricket Team

The Cricket team consists of both male and female. Both teams have won a number of trophies as in 2019, the male team manged to win the national title and the Female team merged the 2nd Runner's Up

 

Trophies Won

The school has won different of trophies among which include Zone Championship, Mukono District Championship, Central Region Championship in the Footbal Team 2019. Mukono District Championship both in the male and female Team, National Runners Up and 2nd Runner's Up in the Male and Female Team Respectively.

 

Second NetBall Team

This NetBall Team has always been kept as a reserve for the 1st Netball Team which has been the winning in Mukono District. The Team has had a number of friendly matches with other teams where the 1st
Team learn from them. This has helped to hide the tactical challenege of the 1st Team making them
No.1

 

VolleyBall Team

The VolleyBall Team has also been doing great in the school sports. It has competed in alot of competitions but unfortunately having less potential in winning, the school is looking at that strategy to find a solution. We a trying very hard to admit more students with such talents and find the best coach to train them at the appropiate time.
